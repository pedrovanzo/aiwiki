# AIWiki

A structured knowledge showcase covering AI fundamentals, systems, and tooling — ordered from core definitions to advanced patterns and personal findings.

## Language

**Knowledge Showcase**:
The intent of this wiki — a curated display of what the author knows and has studied about AI.
_Avoid_: Personal wiki, Study notes, Notes

**Learning Path**:
The organizing principle of the wiki. Content is sequenced top-to-bottom from low to high complexity, within sections and across them.
_Avoid_: Table of contents, Index

**Fundaments**:
The opening section of the learning path. Contains the most basic definitions — what things are, not how to build them.
_Avoid_: Basics, Introduction, Getting Started

**AI Solutions**:
Topics that describe solution patterns, architectures, and pipelines at a planning level — what you can build and how to think about it, not line-by-line implementation.
_Avoid_: Projects, Use cases, Tutorials

**Personal Thoughts**:
The final section. Holds the author's personal opinions, recommendations, and findings — knowledge that is subjective or experiential rather than general-purpose.
_Avoid_: Opinions, Personal notes, Misc

**Muted**:
The visual treatment for topics that exist in the navigation but have no content yet. Visible but dimmed — signals planned depth without breaking the learning path.
_Avoid_: Not covered, Coming soon, Placeholder, Partially covered
